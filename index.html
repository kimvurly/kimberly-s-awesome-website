math

kimberly has loved math starting from the moment she learnt how to do long addition and subtraction. she learnt how to do long addition and subtraction through her mom. she would sit down at the dinner table adding numbers together. sometimes she would also ask her mom's nail clients to give her long numbers so she could play around with them. one of her earliest fond memories is when her mom was giving her numbers to add together, and she accidentally said "five-ty five" instead of "fifty five" :)

as kimberly continued her journey down the road of math, her passion only grew stronger. she never struggled with math until she first starting solving for the infamous x of algebra, and in eighth grade, and got an F on her first algebra quiz. she was so disappointed in herself and vowed to never get such a bad grade again. she studied hard and would write on her whiteboard to review for quizzes and tests. not only did she ace the class, but she never got an F on a math quiz ever again, let alone a B.
